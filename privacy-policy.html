Privacy Policy

This page explains how I collect and use information when you visit Jake Troubleshoots.

Information I Collect

I do not collect personal information directly from visitors. However, some data may be collected automatically through analytics and advertising tools, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic region (country or region level)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is anonymous and aggregated — I cannot identify individual visitors.

Analytics

I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. It collects anonymized usage data through cookies to help me see which guides are most useful and how to improve the site.

You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

Advertising

This site may show ads served by Google AdSense or similar partners. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on previous visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. I only use cookies through third-party analytics and advertising tools. I do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information.

Information You Provide

If you email me at contact@jaketroubleshoots.com, I will only use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry and will not share it with third parties.

How I Use Information

Any analytics data I collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how the site is being used
  • See which guides are most helpful
  • Improve content and site structure over time

I do not sell, rent, or trade information to third parties.

Third-Party Links

The site may link to other websites. I am not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

Children's Privacy

The site is intended for professional use by clinical engineers and biomedical technicians. I do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will appear on this page with a new date.
