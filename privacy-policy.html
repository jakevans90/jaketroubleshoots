Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and handle information when you visit jaketroubleshoots.com (the "Site"). I am committed to being transparent about what I collect and why.

Information I Collect

I do not collect personal information directly from visitors. However, some data may be collected automatically through third-party tools I use to operate and improve the Site:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the Site
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic region (country or region level)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. I cannot identify individual visitors from this information.

Analytics

I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data through cookies. This helps me see which guides are most useful, where traffic comes from, and how to improve the Site.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking via the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

Advertising

The Site may display ads served by Google AdSense or similar partners. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this Site or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. I only use cookies via third-party analytics and advertising tools. I do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information.

Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you email me at contact@jaketroubleshoots.com, I will receive the information you include. I only use it to respond to your message and do not share it with third parties.

How I Use Information

Information collected through analytics is used solely to:

  • Understand how the Site is being used
  • Identify which guides are most helpful
  • Improve Site content and structure over time

Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to external websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Please review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Children's Privacy

This Site is intended for professional use by clinical engineers and biomedical technicians. I do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

If you have questions, email me at contact@jaketroubleshoots.com
