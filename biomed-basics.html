What Is Biomed Basics?

Biomed Basics is a growing section of Jake Troubleshoots focused on the everyday concepts behind biomedical equipment work. The goal is simple: explain what biomeds are actually doing, why the checks matter, and how to think through the work instead of just copying numbers into a form.

These pages are written for BMETs, clinical engineers, students, and healthcare technology staff who want practical explanations without unnecessary textbook language.

Why This Matters

A lot of biomedical equipment work becomes routine: run the PM, get the readings, document the result, and move on. But the better you understand the reason behind each test, the easier it is to catch unsafe conditions, explain failures, troubleshoot weird problems, and know when something needs to be escalated.

Biomed Basics is meant to help close that gap between doing the task and understanding the task.
