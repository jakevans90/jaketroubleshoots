Baxter SIGMA Spectrum

Error: 210 SPI PIC Timeout

Asset Type

Infusion Pump

Manufacturer

Baxter

Model

SIGMA Spectrum

What This Guide Helps With

Error 210 SPI PIC Timeout indicates a loss of communication between the main processor and PIC control circuitry, which can cause the pump to stop, fail to initialize, or become unresponsive during operation.

Step-by-Step Troubleshooting

1. Ensure Patient Safety First

  • Stop infusion immediately
  • Remove the pump from patient use
  • Transfer therapy to a functioning pump

2. Verify the Reported Error and Behavior

  • Power on the pump and confirm Error: 210 SPI PIC Timeout is displayed
  • Note when the error occurs (startup, during infusion, intermittent)
  • Observe for unresponsive controls, failure to start infusion, or rebooting

3. Perform a Power Cycle

  • Turn the pump off completely
  • Disconnect from AC power
  • Wait 30–60 seconds
  • Power the pump back on

4. Check Power Source and Battery Condition

  • Connect to a known good AC outlet
  • Verify charging indicator is present
  • Ensure battery is properly seated and functional

5. Inspect External Connections and Modules

  • Remove and reseat any connected modules or accessories
  • Inspect connectors for damage or contamination
  • Ensure no foreign material is present

6. Attempt Alternate Startup Conditions

  • Power on the pump with no accessories attached
  • Observe if the error persists in a minimal configuration

If the Problem Persists

If Error 210 continues after all external checks:

  • Internal communication between the processor and PIC controller has failed
  • Remove the device from service
  • Label the unit as Out of Service
  • Send for repair or bench evaluation

Clinical Use Tip

Never troubleshoot communication-related errors on an active patient. Always transition the patient to a backup device before evaluating the pump.

Work Order Documentation (CCR Method)

CCR = Complaint, Cause, Resolution

Complaint

What was reported by the clinical staff.

Example:
"Pump displaying Error 210 SPI PIC Timeout; unable to start infusion."

Cause

What was observed during troubleshooting.

Example:
"Internal communication failure suspected after power cycle and external checks did not resolve issue."

Resolution

What action was taken.

Example:
"Removed from service, labeled Out of Service, and sent for repair/bench evaluation."

Helpful Details to Include (If Known)

  • Alarm behavior
  • Accessories swapped
  • Power behavior
  • Environmental factors
  • Indicator lights
  • Final device status

Final Thought

Error 210 SPI PIC Timeout represents a critical internal communication failure. Once external causes are ruled out, proper troubleshooting means escalating the issue promptly to protect patient safety and ensure accurate documentation.

That is successful troubleshooting.
