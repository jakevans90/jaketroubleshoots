Hillrom Centrella Smart+

Error: 0x1E01 SafeView Communication Error

Asset Type

Hospital Bed

Manufacturer

Hillrom

Model

Centrella Smart+

What This Guide Helps With

Error 0x1E01 SafeView Communication Error indicating a communication failure within the SafeView system, leading to loss of bed monitoring features such as patient movement detection and safety alerts.

Step-by-Step Troubleshooting

1. Ensure Patient Safety First

  • Remove the bed from active patient use if SafeView monitoring is required
  • Transfer the patient to a functioning bed if needed

2. Verify the Reported Error and Behavior

  • Confirm Error 0x1E01 is displayed
  • Observe when the error appears (startup or during use)
  • Check if SafeView features are unavailable or intermittent

3. Perform a Basic Power Reset

  • Unplug the bed from AC power
  • Disconnect battery if accessible without disassembly
  • Wait 30–60 seconds
  • Restore power

4. Check External Cables and Connections

  • Inspect accessible cable connections related to control systems
  • Ensure connectors are fully seated
  • Look for damaged, pinched, or loose cables

5. Inspect for Physical Damage or Environmental Issues

  • Check for fluid exposure near control areas
  • Look for signs of impact or stress
  • Inspect for pinched wiring during bed articulation

6. Isolate Potential Cable vs Board Issue

  • Gently manipulate accessible cable routing areas
  • Observe for changes in error behavior

7. Stop if Issue is Resolved

  • If error clears, verify SafeView functionality returns
  • Return bed to service

If the Problem Persists

If Error 0x1E01 continues after external checks, internal communication between system boards is likely failing.

  • Remove device from service
  • Label as Out of Service
  • Send for repair or bench evaluation

Clinical Use Tip

Never troubleshoot on an active patient. Always transfer the patient to a backup bed before attempting troubleshooting when monitoring features are compromised.

Work Order Documentation (CCR Method)

CCR = Complaint, Cause, Resolution

Complaint

What was reported by the clinical staff.

Example:
"Bed displaying Error 0x1E01 SafeView Communication Error; SafeView monitoring unavailable."

Cause

What was observed during troubleshooting.

Example:
"SafeView communication failure persisted after power reset; external connections intact, indicating internal communication fault."

Resolution

What action was taken.

Example:
"Performed reset and inspection with no resolution. Bed removed from service and sent for repair."

Helpful Details to Include (If Known)

  • Alarm behavior
  • Accessories swapped
  • Power behavior
  • Environmental factors
  • Indicator lights
  • Final device status

Final Thought

Communication failures within monitoring systems should always be taken seriously. Rule out simple causes first, then escalate quickly when internal faults are suspected to protect patient safety and system reliability.

That is successful troubleshooting.
