Cardinal Health Kangaroo ePump

Error: 11 Flash Read Error

Asset Type

Feeding Pump

Manufacturer

Cardinal Health

Model

Kangaroo ePump

What This Guide Helps With

Error: 11 Flash Read Error indicates a failure in reading internal memory, which can prevent the pump from starting or functioning correctly. This can result in complete loss of feeding capability and requires prompt evaluation to ensure patient safety.

Step-by-Step Troubleshooting

Ensure Patient Safety First

  • Immediately remove the pump from patient use
  • Transition feeding to a backup pump or method
  • Confirm feeding continues without interruption

Verify the Reported Error and Behavior

  • Power on the pump and observe the display
  • Confirm Error: 11 Flash Read Error is shown
  • Note whether it appears at startup or during operation
  • Check if the device is frozen or non-functional

Perform a Power Cycle

  • Turn the pump OFF
  • Disconnect from AC power
  • Remove and reinsert the battery if accessible
  • Wait 30–60 seconds
  • Reconnect power and restart the pump

Confirm Power Source Stability

  • Plug the device into a known-good outlet
  • Verify the power adapter connection is secure
  • Observe for consistent startup behavior

Inspect for Physical or Environmental Issues

  • Check for signs of liquid intrusion
  • Inspect housing for cracks or impact damage
  • Ensure the device is within normal temperature conditions

If the Problem Persists

If Error: 11 Flash Read Error continues after completing all external troubleshooting steps, internal causes have been confirmed.

  • Remove the device from service
  • Label the device as Out of Service
  • Send the unit for repair or control board replacement

Clinical Use Tip

Never attempt to continue using a pump that intermittently clears this error. Memory-related faults can lead to unpredictable operation and unsafe feeding delivery. Always transition the patient to a backup device first.

Work Order Documentation (CCR Method)

CCR = Complaint, Cause, Resolution

Complaint

What was reported by the clinical staff.

Example:
"Pump displaying Error: 11 Flash Read Error and will not start."

Cause

What was observed during troubleshooting.

Example:
"Internal flash memory read failure confirmed after repeated power cycles; error persisted at startup."

Resolution

What action was taken.

Example:
"Removed device from service and sent for repair due to suspected control board failure."

Helpful Details to Include (If Known)

  • Alarm behavior
  • Accessories swapped
  • Power behavior
  • Environmental factors
  • Indicator lights
  • Final device status

Final Thought

Error: 11 Flash Read Error is a strong indicator of internal memory failure. Efficient troubleshooting focuses on verifying the error, ruling out external causes, and escalating quickly to protect patient safety and reduce downtime.

That is successful troubleshooting.
