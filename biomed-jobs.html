Job Titles to Search

Searching only for “biomed” can miss a lot of openings. Try these titles and variations:

  • Biomedical Equipment Technician — common hospital BMET title.
  • BMET — short version used by many facilities and staffing sites.
  • Clinical Engineering Technician — common in hospitals and health systems.
  • Healthcare Technology Management Technician — sometimes used by larger HTM programs.
  • Medical Equipment Technician — broader title that may include biomed work.
  • Field Service Engineer — common OEM/vendor title for medical equipment service.
  • Imaging Service Engineer — CT, MRI, X-ray, ultrasound, nuclear medicine, and other imaging service roles.
  • Dialysis Biomedical Technician — dialysis equipment service roles.
  • Sterilizer Service Technician — sterile processing equipment service roles.
  • Anesthesia Service Technician — anesthesia machine and OR equipment service roles.

Where Else to Look

Job boards help, but biomed openings are often hidden inside hospital career portals or posted under unexpected titles.

  • Hospital and health system career sites: search for biomed, biomedical, clinical engineering, equipment technician, HTM, and field service.
  • OEM career pages: search for field service engineer, customer service engineer, imaging engineer, and medical equipment service.
  • ISO and HTM service companies: search for BMET, technician, site manager, imaging engineer, and clinical engineering roles.
  • Dialysis companies: search for biomedical technician, dialysis equipment technician, and biomed roles.
  • Local universities and research hospitals: search for clinical engineering, lab equipment, biomedical electronics, or research equipment service.

Entry-Level Biomed Job Search Tips

If you are trying to break into the field, do not search only for perfect BMET I openings. Search related titles too.

  • Look for BMET I, Biomedical Equipment Technician I, and Clinical Engineering Technician I.
  • Search for medical equipment technician and field service technician.
  • Look at dialysis equipment jobs, sterile processing equipment jobs, and rental equipment jobs.
  • Highlight electronics troubleshooting, mechanical repair, customer service, documentation, and safety testing experience.
  • Do not ignore hospital career sites. Some positions never show up clearly on general job boards.
